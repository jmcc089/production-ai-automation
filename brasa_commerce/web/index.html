Our Story

Founded in Chicago, built for the long haul

Brasa started in 2021 with a simple frustration: cookware that wears out faster than the meals it makes. We wanted pans that improve with age — that carry the memory of every sear and simmer.

Each piece is hand-finished and pre-seasoned in small batches. No nonstick coatings to flake, no planned obsolescence. Just honest tools that reward the cooks who use them.
