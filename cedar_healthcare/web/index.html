The practitioners

Small team,
deep expertise

Three licensed specialists who collaborate directly on your care — no referral maze, no front desk delay.

AF
Dr. Ana Fuentes
Physiotherapy · DPT

Dr. Fuentes brings over a decade of clinical experience in orthopaedic and sports rehabilitation. She uses a movement-first approach, combining manual therapy with targeted exercise to restore function and prevent re-injury. Fluent in English and Spanish.

MR
Miguel Rivas
Sports massage · LMT

Miguel is a licensed massage therapist with a background in competitive athletics. His sessions are precise and outcome-focused — built around what the body actually needs to recover and perform. He works closely with Dr. Fuentes on shared patients.

CM
Carla Mendoza
Clinical nutrition · RD

Carla is a registered dietitian specialising in metabolic health, gut microbiome, and performance nutrition. She partners with patients to translate complex lab data into practical, sustainable dietary changes — no generic meal plans.
