What We Do

Our Practice Areas

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Visa

Reunite with your loved ones. We handle immediate relative petitions, family preference categories, and consular processing for spouses, children, and parents of U.S. citizens and permanent residents.

  • I-130 Petition for Alien Relative
  • I-864 Affidavit of Support
  • DS-260 Immigrant Visa Application
  • Medical Exam (I-693)
💼
EB Green Card

Employment-based permanent residency for professionals, executives, and skilled workers. We guide you through the I-140 petition, priority date strategy, and I-485 adjustment of status.

  • I-140 Immigrant Petition
  • I-485 Adjustment of Status
  • PERM Labor Certification
  • Educational Credential Evaluation
🛡️
Asylum

Protection for those fleeing persecution. We represent asylum seekers in affirmative and defensive proceedings, building comprehensive cases that document the fear and risk you face in your home country.

  • I-589 Application for Asylum
  • Personal Statement Preparation
  • Country Condition Evidence
  • USCIS & Immigration Court Representation
🇺🇸
Naturalization

Become a U.S. citizen. We prepare and file your N-400 application, ensure your eligibility requirements are met, and prepare you thoroughly for the civics interview and test.

  • N-400 Application for Naturalization
  • Eligibility Assessment
  • Interview Preparation
  • Oath Ceremony Coordination
